Emerald City Weightlifting

Emerald City is a weightlifting gym in North Seattle. Weightlifting, also known as Olympic weightlifting, tests athletes in two barbell lifts: the Snatch, and the Clean & Jerk.

We love lifting and having fun! Emerald City Weightlifting is a place to learn, hone and perfect your form, no matter what your level.
